# STAR Story - SQL Decision Analysis

## Situation
A product team had rising sales but unclear profit quality across channels and customer segments.

## Task
Deliver a fast, defensible recommendation on where to invest and where to cut spend.

## Action
- Built a SQL analysis workflow over transactional data
- Calculated monthly trend, margin quality, return risk, and customer concentration
- Translated results into a short decision memo for leadership

## Result
Identified that Enterprise + Partner should be prioritized, while SMB Paid Search needed discount controls. The plan improves margin quality and reduces return leakage without stopping growth.
